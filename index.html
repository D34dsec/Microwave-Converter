Microwave Cooking Time Converter

This Microwave Cooking Time Converter helps you easily adjust cooking times for microwave meals based on different wattages. Follow these simple steps to convert your meal's cooking time:

  1. Enter the original cooking time (in minutes and seconds) provided in the meal's instructions.
  2. Enter the wattage specified in the meal's instructions.
  3. Enter the wattage of the microwave you will be using.
  4. Click "Calculate" to get the new cooking time based on your microwave's wattage.
